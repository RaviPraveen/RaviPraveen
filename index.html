Fresh Grocers System(Desktop Web App UI/UX For Customers,CSR,Delivery agents)

The Fresh Grocers System is a modern web-based grocery ordering and delivery platform designed to make online grocery shopping simple, fast, and convenient for customers. The system connects three main users — Customers, Delivery Agents, and Customer Service Representatives (CSR) — under one digital platform that ensures smooth and reliable operations from ordering to delivery. For customers, the system allows them to register, browse products by category, add items to their cart, and complete their orders through an easy checkout process. After placing an order, customers can track the delivery status in real time and receive SMS or email notifications. They can also manage their profile, view order history, and provide feedback to improve the service experience. For delivery agents, the system provides a dedicated dashboard where they can log in to view assigned deliveries, accept or reject orders, update delivery progress, and mark orders as completed. This ensures efficient route management and communication between agents and customers. For customer service representatives (CSRs), the system offers tools to manage customer orders, assign delivery agents, monitor delivery status, and handle complaints or feedback. CSRs can also generate reports on performance and customer satisfaction to help improve operations. The Fresh Grocers System has been designed with a user-friendly interface, responsive layout, and modern UI/UX principles using soft color themes and clear navigation. It ensures that every user — whether a customer or staff member — can perform their tasks easily and effectively. Overall, this system demonstrates the use of structured design, database management, and process automation to create a professional and reliable e-commerce solution tailored for the grocery industry. It highlights strong technical and design skills in front-end development, database handling, and user experience design, making it a valuable project for both academic and professional portfolios.
